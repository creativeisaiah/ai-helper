AstroBot
Welcome! Meet AstroBot! It is a helpful companion and can provide assistance with whatever you need. Feel free to ask it anything, but understand that as an early A.I. model, it is not perfect, and some answers may be false, so always double check information. The conversation startup may take a few seconds while the server starts up so please be patient. Also, this is just 1 instance of AstroBot and if the page refreshes, the conversation will start be erased as there are no databanks to store conversations.
